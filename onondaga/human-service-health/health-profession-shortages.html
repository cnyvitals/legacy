Health Profession Shortages

What does this indicator measure?

This indicator shows the number of health profession shortages per 100 in Albany, Monroe, Onondaga, and Erie counties.


Source: Designated HPSA Statistics, U.S. Department of Health & Human Services, Office of Shortage Designation
